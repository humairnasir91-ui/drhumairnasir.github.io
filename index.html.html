About the Doctor

Clinical Precision.
Natural Results.

Dr. Humair Nasir is a DHA-licensed Aesthetic Physician and Medical Director at GS Poly Clinic, Jumeirah, Dubai. With over 7 years of hands-on expertise across Dubai and Pakistan, he specialises in advanced non-surgical cosmetic procedures with a philosophy centered on natural, results-driven outcomes.

Trained under internationally recognised cosmetic surgery expert Dr. Ifran Shaikh (MBBS, FCPS, FRCS, RCPSG), Dr. Humair brings both scientific precision and artistic sensibility to every treatment. He is known among patients for his thoroughness in consultation, patience during procedures, and exceptional care throughout the treatment journey.

As Medical Director, he leads clinical governance, quality assurance, and regulatory compliance at GS Poly Clinic — ensuring every patient receives the highest standard of care in a safe, professional environment.
